Articles:

See below links to my most recent and most successful analyses that have been published on TowardsDataScience.com. They all take the form of an article, focussing more on interesting patterns and explanations, rather than opinion or commentary.

Game of Thrones Season 8: What went wrong?

Using Data Science to analyse why the final season of Game of Thrones was so unpopular and how it differed so drastically from previous seasons.

A Game of Words: Season 8

The follow up to my Game of Words series, where I analyse the scripts of Season 8 of Game of Thrones.

The Political Twittersphere of the UK

The first part of a trilogy examining the UK government as it is on Twitter. In this first article I take a look at which MPs are on Twitter and who has the most followers. The second and third part investigate this in more detail.

A Game of Words

My most popular blog post, the beginning of a trilogy where I firstly examine transcripts from the TV series A Game of Thrones and show that the words characters use can perfectly reflect their personality. Then I perform some survival analysis to see what makes characters in Game of Thrones more likely to die.
